KV Compression Axes — What Information is Preserved

Q Compression Axes — What Queries Ask For

SVD of q_a_proj (1536 dims). These are the shared query directions. Axes marked DIVERGENT have <50% token overlap between models — the backdoor altered these query directions.

RoPE (Positional) Axes

SVD of the RoPE portion (64 dims) of kv_a_proj_with_mqa. These encode positional information. The token projections here are less semantically meaningful — they show which tokens tend to appear at similar positions during training.
